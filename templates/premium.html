Step-by-Step User Guide

1. Homepage Access

After logging in, you will see this screen. Click the “Let’s Start” button to begin uploading your media.

2. Upload Photo or Video

Select a photo or video file and click the “Upload” button to start the people detection process.

3. View Detection Results

The system will detect and count people automatically and display the results on your screen.

4. Live Camera Detection

Click the “Start Camera Detection” button to start real-time people detection via your webcam.

5. IP Camera Detection

Enter the IP camera stream URL and click the "Start IP Camera Detection" button to detect people in real-time from an IP camera feed.
